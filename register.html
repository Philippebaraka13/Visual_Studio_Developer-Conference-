Annual Conference

May 23-25, 2024
VSLive! Developer Conference has been training developers, software architects, engineers, and designers like you since 1993. As we celebrate our 30th Anniversary, we return to the Tech Mecca, Microsoft Headquarters in Redmond, WA for five days of in-depth training, networking, and fun! Sharpen your skills in Microsoft Visual Studio 2022, .NET 7, DevOps, MAUI, JavaScript, TypeScript, Angular, Blazor, and modern data processing techniques. Our speakers will answer your questions with practical guidance on native client development, web, database, and so much more. Explore everything Microsoft HQ and the Pacific Northwest have to offer and further your education at the same time!
